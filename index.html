About Me Will Cooke

With 8 years of coaching experience, I bring a wealth of knowledge and a genuine passion for the game to every session. Specialising in junior development, I have a natural ability to connect with young players — making learning fun, building confidence, and instilling a love of tennis that lasts a lifetime. That said, my expertise doesn't stop there. Whether you're picking up a racket for the very first time or looking to sharpen your game as an amateur, I tailor my coaching style to suit you. I have a knack for breaking the game down into simple, digestible steps — making me an ideal coach for beginners who want to feel at ease from the very first lesson.

I hold an LTA (Lawn Tennis Association) Coaching Instructor qualification and have worked with juniors and adults across a variety of different skill levels. Every session is purposeful, energetic, and — most importantly — fun.
